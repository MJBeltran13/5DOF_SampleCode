Manual Control Instructions:

  • Left Stick: Control X,Y position
  • A (Button 0): Z-axis Up (+10mm)
  • X (Button 2): Z-axis Down (-10mm)
  • B (Button 1): Yaw Right (+10°)
  • Y (Button 3): Yaw Left (-10°)
  • LB (Button 4): Rotate Gripper Left (-10°)
  • RB (Button 5): Rotate Gripper Right (+10°)
  • Button 9: Toggle Gripper Open/Close
  • Back (Button 6): Exit Manual Mode
